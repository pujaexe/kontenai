About Konten.ai

Konten.ai is an AI-powered content agency based in Gianyar, Bali, Indonesia. We specialize in done-for-you social media management and content automation for property businesses including villas, boutique hotels, and guesthouses, as well as SMEs in food and beverage, fashion, and retail sectors.

Our AI Agent System

We deploy four interconnected AI agents: a Research Agent that analyzes your business and competitors, a Trend Agent that monitors Instagram and TikTok trends, a Content Agent that writes captions and copy in your brand voice, and a Posting Agent that schedules and publishes automatically to Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook.

Services

  • AI Social Media Management for Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook
  • Property Content for Villas, Hotels, and Guesthouses in Bali
  • Automated Lead Generation via WhatsApp
  • Monthly Content Strategy and Analytics
  • SME Content for Food, Fashion, and Retail
  • AI Visual Production and Mockups

Pricing

  • Starter Plan: Rp 500,000 per month — 12 to 15 posts, AI captions, Instagram and Facebook, monthly report
  • Growth Engine Plan: Rp 2,000,000 per month — 20 to 25 posts, Stories and Reels, auto DM replies, WhatsApp lead gen, TikTok included, monthly strategy call
  • Full Agency Plan: Rp 4,000,000 per month — everything in Growth Engine plus AI video Reels, Google review management, OTA copywriting, dedicated account manager, weekly analytics

No setup fee on any plan. Cancel anytime.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I go live with Konten.ai?
Within 14 hours after onboarding. We handle all setup.

No. Zero setup fee on all plans.
What platforms does Konten.ai post to?
Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook. WhatsApp automation is also included in Growth Engine and above.
Is Konten.ai suitable for Bali villas and hotels?
Yes. We specialize in hospitality property content including seasonal promotions, room highlights, OTA listings, and automated guest review responses.
